What We Do

InstaTute is an online community that connects students with peer tutors who have excelled in similar courses. Our main goal is to provide timely help to students while keeping the hours flexible and the information current. We are committed to work within the busy college lifestyle to make tutoring at school more accessible to all while building strong campus unity and student involvement.
